IEA World Energy Outlook Scenarios

STEPS — Stated Policies Scenario
Reflects existing government policies and measures as of mid-2024. Represents a baseline projection of where the energy system is heading based on current policy settings.
APS — Announced Pledges Scenario
Assumes all national energy and climate targets made by governments are met in full and on time, including Nationally Determined Contributions under the Paris Agreement.
NZE — Net Zero Emissions by 2050
Maps out a pathway for the global energy sector to achieve net zero CO₂ emissions by 2050. Consistent with limiting global warming to 1.5°C (with no or limited overshoot).

Source: IEA World Energy Outlook 2024
